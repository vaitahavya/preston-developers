## 7. Content Structure for Digital Marketing

### **Landing Page Flow:**
1. **Hero + Value Proposition** (Above fold)
2. **Villa Features** (Scannable cards)
3. **Clubhouse Virtual Tour** (Video/Interactive)
4. **Amenities Grid** (Visual categories)
5. **Lifestyle Benefits** (Story-driven)
6. **Investment Opportunity** (ROI focused)
7. **Location Advantages** (Map + connectivity)
8. **Social Proof** (Testimonials/awards)
9. **Call-to-Action** (Contact/Visit)

### **Content Priorities:**
1. **Mobile-First** design and content hierarchy
2. **Visual-Heavy** with minimal text per section
3. **Benefit-Led** headlines over feature lists
4. **Action-Oriented** CTAs throughout
5. **Trust-Building** elements (certifications, awards)
